WEB-SAFE FONTS

These are fonts that work on most computers. If a web-safe font is either your specified font or one of your fallbacks, your webpages will display correctly on the majority of computers.

Examples of fonts that exist on most computer systems are:

  • Arial
  • Helvetica
  • Times
  • Times New Roman
  • Courier New
  • Courier

With mobile devices, however, the list of web-safe fonts is far smaller.
